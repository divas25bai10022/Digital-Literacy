# My "**Stay Safe**" Checklist







1. *Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking*. Use your mobile data for UPI or bank logins. Public Wi-Fi is way too easy to "sniff" for data.

2. *Frequently check your "Logged-in Devices"*.

3. If you are targeted, don't wait. Immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal *(cybercrime.gov.in)* or call the *24/7 Helpline at 1930*.

4. Remember that you *never need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.*

5. we must *treat our university credentials like bank details*. Never post a photo of your ID card or hall ticket online, as these contain the exact numbers identity thieves are looking for.

6. *stop being honest with security questions*; instead of using your real high school, use a random word or a movie title that a stranger could never find on your profile.

7. *Enable Two-Factor Authentication* on your college email and all social accounts.

8. *Make your main social media accounts private*. Only share "Day in the Life" content with people you actually know in person.
